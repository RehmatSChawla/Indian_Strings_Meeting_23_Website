About This Event

What is the ISM?

Indian Strings Meeting is a prestigious biennial meeting with national and international participation.

What is the ISM for?

The plan of this intensive one-week meeting will be to invite scientists (both Indian and foreign) working in topical areas to give plenary lectures as well as focused 30 minute seminars.

What are the benefits?

Indian Strings Meeting 2023 aims at organizing talks which can potentially lead to new ideas and collaboration
